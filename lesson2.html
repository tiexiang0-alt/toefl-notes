Writing Heat Map

Topic:

Some people prefer to study alone, while others prefer to study with a group. Which do you prefer?

George's Sample (250 words):

I prefer to study with a group. Firstly, group study is efficient. When we encounter difficult problems, partners can give us suggestions. For example, last week, my classmate taught me a math formula. Secondly, studying together makes the process interesting. We can discuss various topics during breaks. In conclusion, I find group study helpful because it keeps me focused and happy.

Describe a memorable gathering with your friends.
