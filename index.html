help 1. 什么是句子？

lightbulb

句子是至少有一个主语和一个动词，并且能够表述 完整意义 的词的组合。

反例：碎片 (Fragments)

❌ ...wants to go to Harvard. (缺主语：谁想去？)

❌ George, to major in Chemistry. (缺谓语：想去还是要去？)

❌ George loves... (意义不完整：爱谁？)

person 2. 主语 (Subject)

句子叙述的主体，通常位于句首。核心特征：必须是名词性质。

badge 1. 名词/代词 expand_more

最常见的形式。人名、地名、代词。

  • Papa and George love feeding bread to ducks.
  • She left without a word.
move_up 2. 不定式 & 动名词 expand_more

动词不能直接做主语，必须"变身"为名词性质。

  • To see is to believe.
    不定式 (To do)：眼见为实。
  • Seeing is believing.
    Smoking is bad for health.
    动名词 (Doing)：抽烟有害健康。
featured_play_list 3. 主语从句 expand_more

整个句子充当主语。

What he said is not the truth.

"他所说的" 这件事不是事实。

bolt 3. 谓语 (Predicate)

对主语动作或状态的陈述。形式多样：

简单动词 She left without a word.
动词短语 George jumped into the muddy puddles.
情态动词 George can play the ukulele.
助动词 George doesn't agree with Papa.

adjust 4. 宾语 (Object)

普通宾语 expand_more

I hope to see you again. (不定式做宾语)

They are talking about the school beauty. (介词宾语，谈论校花)

diversity_3 双宾语 (重点) expand_more

给某人某物，买某人某物。通常 人(间接) + 物(直接)。

  • Give the dog some food.
  • He bought his girlfriend some flowers.

link 5. 表语 (Predicative)

配合 系动词 (Link Verb) 使用，说明主语的性质或状态。

visibility 感官动词 (Sense Verbs) - 最重要！
expand_more
warning 注意：感官动词没有被动语态！不要说 "is felt"。
Look

You look beautiful.

Feel

It feels good.

Taste

It tastes delicious.

Smell

palette

定语 (Attribute)

修饰名词/代词。翻译为“...的”。

expand_more

定语不仅可以是形容词，还可以是词组或句子，用来限定“是哪一个”。

形容词
The lazy boy.
副词
The man over there is my dad.
介词短语
The woman with a baby is my aunt.
哪个女人？抱孩子的那个。
过去分词
The tree planted last year is growing well.
哪棵树？去年种的那棵。
不定式
I have an idea to solve the problem.
timer

状语 (Adverbial)

修饰动词/全句。说明时间、地点、原因、条件、目的、让步等。

expand_more

状语非常考验逻辑，它说明了动作发生的背景。

地点 Place

George lives on the 1st floor.

说明住的状态。

时间 Time

George goes to school at 6:00.

原因 Reason

George was late because the alarm didn't work.

条件 Condition

George will feed ducks if it doesn't rain.

加上"不下雨"的条件。

目的 Purpose

In order to lose weight, George didn't have dinner.

为了达到减肥的目的。

让步 Concession

George is doing homework though he is sleepy.

"虽然...但是..." (尽管很困，还在做).

add_circle 7. 补语 (Complement)

lightbulb

秘诀：补谁就是什么补。补主语叫主补，补宾语叫宾补。

主语补足语 (Subject Comp)

George was found singing.

George 被发现(在唱歌)。Singing 补充说明 George。

宾语补足语 (Object Comp)

We found George singing.

我们发现 George (在唱歌)。Singing 补充说明宾语 George。

更多宾补例子：

  • George found the bonbon box empty. (形容词补语: 发现糖盒是空的)
  • George saw his face reflected in the water. (过去分词补语: 看见脸倒映在水里)

Everything starts here. 就像音符构成了乐章。
